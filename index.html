ABOUT US

At Katunda Studio, we believe that every moment in life is special and deserves to be captured in its full beauty. That's why we offer a wide range of photography and videography services to help our clients celebrate and preserve their precious memories. Our studio was founded in 2021 and is located in the heart of Gongo la Mboto, Majohe. Since then, we have built a trusted customer base that has made us their go-to destination for professional photography and videography services.

Our team of talented photographers and videographers are passionate about their craft and always strive to exceed our clients' expectations. We use state-of-the-art equipment and innovative techniques to capture stunning images and create timeless videos that tell a story. Whether it's an event photoshoot, video recording, portrait session, indoor or outdoor photoshoot, family photoshoot, or maternity photoshoot, we work with our clients to tailor our services to their unique needs.

We understand that every client is different, and that's why we take the time to get to know our clients and their vision. Our personalized approach allows us to create a comfortable and fun environment where our clients can relax and be themselves. We believe that this is the key to capturing natural and authentic moments that our clients can cherish for a lifetime.

At Katunda Studio, our motto is "We capture it all!" and we take that very seriously. We know that life moves fast and that every moment is precious. That's why we're committed to providing our clients with the highest quality photography and videography services to help them celebrate and preserve their memories. So whether it's a wedding, a graduation, a family gathering, or simply a day in the park, we're here to help you capture it all!
